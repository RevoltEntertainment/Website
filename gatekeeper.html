Gameplay & Features

Inspect, Detect, Decide

Enforce the Protocol

Examine each visitor carefully. Check their belongings, listen to their statements, and compare everything against the Entry Validation Protocol. Small details can reveal deception.

15-Shift Story with Multiple Endings

Every decision has consequences. Your choices throughout the 15 shifts will determine your reputation and ultimately decide which of the multiple endings you will experience.

Upgrade Your Apartment

Between shifts, you return to your apartment. Spend your earnings to upgrade your living space and unlock new tools and information. Your life beyond the checkpoint evolves with you.
