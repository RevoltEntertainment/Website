Gameplay & Features

Atmospheric Folk Horror

Explore Pradonin

Navigate dense forests, forgotten chapels, and decaying huts, each connected to a different character's sin in a richly detailed Unreal Engine 5 world.

Day/Night Cycle

Day: Gather candles, ritual tools, and stealth aids. Night: Survive the hunt. The Leshy roams. Sound and light are your enemies—or your only hope.

3D Audio Experience

Built for immersion. Headphones are recommended—the Leshy hears everything. Find journal entries and cursed relics to uncover the downfall.
