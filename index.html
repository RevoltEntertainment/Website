Who We Are

Based in Tbilisi, Georgia, Revolt Entertainment is a passionate team of developers, artists, and storytellers dedicated to crafting immersive gaming experiences.

We specialize in psychological horror and narrative-driven games, drawing heavy inspiration from Eastern European folklore and history. Our mission is to push the boundaries of atmospheric storytelling.
